Circuit Training

The main type of workout for our app is circuit Training. But what is circuit training any way? Well simply put it is simply doing a set of exercises back to back with little to no rest and then repeating the cycle over and over again The benefits of these types of workouts include shorter workout time, increase levels of strength and aerobic fitness, as well as a higher calorie burn compared to a regular workout.

A SAMPLE WORKOUT

warm-up/stretch
pull-ups 10 reps
sit-ups 1min
push-ups 20 reps
squats 15 reps
repeat 4 times
Note: always remember to warm-up and stretch before any physical activity.
